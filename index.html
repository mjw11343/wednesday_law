About Wednesday Law

Founded in 2024 by Attorney Michael Williams, Wednesday Law is dedicated to providing empathetic legal representation, and education. The firm provides family law, estate planning, Indigenous law, and legal services for organizations and individuals. Wednesday Law delivers reliable legal representation tailored to individuals. The firm is named after Attorney Williams' dog, Wednesday, who supported Williams through his legal and collegiate education.

With our limited staff, services will be provided on a case-by-case basis.

Practice Areas

Family Law

We handle divorce, child custody, placement and support, guardianships, mediation, and more to help families navigate difficult times with care and compassion.

Estate Planning

Our estate planning services include wills and power of attorney, ensuring your wishes are respected and your loved ones are protected.

Indigenous Law

We offer consultations on Indigenous law issues, providing reliable information on the rapidly changing fields of Federal Indian Law, Tribal Law, and Indigenous Tradition and Customs.

Legal Services for Organizations

We provide legal services for small businesses, nonprofits, and individuals interested in developing their own organizations.
